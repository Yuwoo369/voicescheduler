Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I add an event with voice?

A: Simply tap the microphone button and speak naturally. For example, say "Team meeting tomorrow at 3 PM" and the AI will automatically extract the date, time, and event details.

Q: Which languages are supported?

A: VoiceScheduler supports 7 languages: Korean, English, Japanese, Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, Spanish, and French.

Q: How do I connect my Google Calendar?

A: Tap "Continue with Google" on the login screen and authorize calendar access. Your events will automatically sync with Google Calendar.

Q: What is Smart Time Slot?

A: Smart Time Slot analyzes your focus patterns and calendar availability to recommend the optimal time for your tasks. Tap the brain icon to see AI recommendations.

Q: Is my voice data stored?

A: No. Voice is processed on-device using Apple's speech recognition. We do not store or transmit your voice recordings.

Q: How do I change the app language?

A: The app follows your device language settings. Change your iPhone language in Settings > General > Language & Region.

Q: Can I edit events after they're created?

A: Yes! Tap on any event card to edit the title, time, or priority before registering to your calendar.

Q: What's the daily limit for free users?

A: Free users can create up to 3 schedules per day. Premium users get 30 schedules per day with faster AI processing.

Troubleshooting

Voice recognition not working?

Make sure you've granted microphone and speech recognition permissions in Settings > VoiceScheduler. Also check that you're in a quiet environment.

Events not syncing to Google Calendar?

Try logging out and logging back in with your Google account. Make sure you have an internet connection when registering events.

Contact us at support@voicescheduler.app
